Shri guru granth sahib ji (SGGS) is a holy scripture for the sikhs from 1755. sggs teaches us the way of living the life.
As per Sggs, the main goal of the life is to make your life truthfull and live according to the will of the almighty. Japji Sahib The first bani in Sggs by Guru Nanak sahib is the core of gurbani .The teaching of writtings of sggs is known as gurbani. There are total 1430 pages of sggs known as angs (limbs) The Sggs includes writings of 6 Gurus (From 1st guru to 5th guru and 9th guru) and 26 bhagats. The gurbani is written in the form of raags. There are 31 raags in total. This was some information on Sggs.
